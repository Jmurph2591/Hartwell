Our Services


We provide emergency service for some of our local cities, such as the cities of Southfield, Ferndale, and Allen Park. We would be pleased to provide the same for your company if you should require such service.




Why Choose Us

Hartwell Cement places and repairs concrete: flat work, curbing, catch basins, sewers, driveways, approaches, handicap ramps, dock levelers, truck wells/docks, scale/machine foundations and/or pits ect.

We are well supplied with a variety of equipment such as pavers, backhoes, breakers, bulldozers, concrete saws, dump trucks, and various other tools that we use in the course of our work.

We are a qualified and competitive concrete company with all of our employees being union affiliated and experts in their field of work.
